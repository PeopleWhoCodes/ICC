About ICC


Infinite Clone Conspiracy is a marvel-ous open-source program envisioned by Techsahead. Its mission is to educate learners about open-source programming and illustrate the power of real-time unified problem-solving. The project that we will be hosting have been hand-picked to encourage everyone to think creatively and collaborate.


The students will be guided by qualified mentors throughout their power packed journey. They'll learn the skills they need to succeed in the programming industry while also learning a lot about open-source.
